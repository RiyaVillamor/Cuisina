prep time

10 min.

cook time

45 min.

serving

6 servings

Tags : beef seafood veggies pork

instructions

step 1

In a pressure cooker, boil one quart of water. Add tripe and oxtail. Boiling should continue for 20 minutes at medium heat. Don't cover the cooker.

step 2

After boiling the beef, drain the water. Fill the pressure cooker with 1 quart of fresh water. 25 minutes of pressure cooking with a cover.

step 3

Mix 1 cup of boiling water with annatto seeds to make annatto water. Stir. 10 minutes of seed soaking. By filtering with a kitchen sieve, remove the seeds. Set aside.

ingredients

3 lbs oxtail cut in 2 inch slices you an also use tripe or beef slices

1 piece small banana flower bud sliced

1 bundle pechay or bok choy

1 bundle string beans cut into 2 inch slices

4 pieces eggplants sliced

1 cup ground peanuts

1/2 cup peanut butter

1/2 cup shrimp paste

34 Ounces water about 1 Liter

1/2 cup annatto seeds soaked in a cup of water

1/2 cup toasted ground rice

1 tbsp garlic minced

1/2 cup toasted ground rice

salt and pepper
