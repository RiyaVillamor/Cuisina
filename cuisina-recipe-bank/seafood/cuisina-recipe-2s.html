Ginataang Tambakol

Ginataang Tambakol is a popular Filipino dish known for its rich and creamy coconut milk sauce.

prep time

15 min.

cook time

20 min.

serving

1 serving

Tags : seafood

instructions

step 1

Heat a pan over medium heat and add cooking oil. Sauté the ginger, garlic, and onion until fragrant.

step 2

Add the tambakol steak to the pan and sear each side for about 2 minutes or until slightly browned.

step 3

Pour in the coconut milk and water. Add the green chili pepper (if using), salt, and pepper. Stir gently to combine.

step 4

Bring the mixture to a simmer and let it cook for about 15 minutes or until the tambakol steak is cooked through and tender.

step 5

Taste the sauce and adjust the seasoning if needed.
