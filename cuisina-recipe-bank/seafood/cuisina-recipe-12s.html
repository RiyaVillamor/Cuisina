Sinigang na Hipon

Sinigang na Hipon is a beloved Filipino soup known for its tangy and savory flavors. Tamarind, the key ingredient, adds a unique sourness to the dish.

prep time

10 min.

cook time

20 min.

serving

1 serving

Tags : seafood

instructions

step 1

In a pot, bring the water to a boil over medium heat.

step 2

Add the onion and tomato to the pot. Let them simmer for a few minutes until they soften and release their flavors.

step 3

Stir in the tamarind soup base or sinigang mix, ensuring it dissolves in the water.

step 4

Add the radish, eggplant, and green chili pepper to the pot. Simmer for about 5 minutes or until the vegetables start to become tender.

step 5

Gently add the shrimp to the pot and cook for 3-5 minutes or until they turn pink and are fully cooked.

step 6

Add the okra to the pot and simmer for an additional 2 minutes or until the okra is cooked but still slightly crisp.

step 7

Season the sinigang with fish sauce or salt according to taste. Adjust the seasoning as desired.

step 8

Remove from heat and transfer the Sinigang na Hipon to a serving bowl

step 9

Garnish with fresh cilantro or spinach leaves (if desired) for added freshness and aroma.
