Sinaing na Tulingan

Sinaing na Tulingan is a traditional Filipino dish from Batangas, known for its slow-cooked method.

prep time

15 min.

cook time

1 hr and 30 min.

serving

1 serving

Tags : seafood

instructions

step 1

In a pot/casserole, combine water, vinegar, soy sauce, minced garlic, chopped onion, and dried bay leaves. Stir well.

step 2

Place the Tulingan steak in the pot and bring the mixture to a boil over medium heat.

step 3

Once boiling, lower the heat to a simmer and cover the pot.

step 4

Let the Tulingan cook for about 1 hour and 30 minutes or until the fish is tender and fully cooked.

step 5

Occasionally check and skim off any foam or impurities that may rise to the surface.

step 6

Season with salt and pepper to taste. Adjust the seasoning as desired.

step 7

Once cooked, carefully remove the Tulingan from the pot and transfer it to a serving plate.

step 8

Serve the Sinaing na Tulingan hot with steamed rice and a dipping sauce of your choice.
