Sweet & Sour Tilapia

The dish is known for its vibrant colors and delightful balance of flavors, making it a favorite among seafood lovers in the Philippines.

prep time

15 min.

cook time

20 min.

serving

1 serving

Tags : seafood

instructions

step 1

Season the tilapia fillet with salt and pepper. Coat it evenly with cornstarch.

step 2

Heat the cooking oil in a pan over medium-high heat. Fry the tilapia fillet until golden brown on both sides. Set it aside.

step 3

In the same pan, saut√© the onion and garlic until fragrant and translucent.

step 4

Add the bell peppers and pineapple chunks to the pan and cook for a few minutes until slightly softened.

step 5

In a separate bowl, mix together the vinegar, ketchup, soy sauce, brown sugar, and a pinch of salt and pepper. Stir until well combined.

step 6

Pour the sauce mixture into the pan with the vegetables. Stir and let it simmer for a few minutes until the sauce thickens slightly.

step 7

Add the fried tilapia fillet to the pan, gently coating it with the sauce.

step 8

Cook for another minute or two, allowing the fish to absorb the flavors.

step 9

Garnish with green onions (optional) and serve the Sweet & Sour Tilapia hot with steamed rice.
