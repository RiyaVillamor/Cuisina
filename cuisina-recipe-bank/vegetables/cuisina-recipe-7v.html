Patola with Miswa

Patola with Miswa is a comforting Filipino soup that combines the mild flavor and crunch of loofah with thin rice noodles.

prep time

10 min.

cook time

25 min.

serving

1 serving

Tags : veggies

instructions

step 1

Heat the cooking oil in a pot over medium heat.

step 2

Saut√© the onion and garlic until fragrant and translucent.

step 3

If using ground pork or chicken, add it to the pot and cook until browned. (It can be topped with meatballs and sardines alternatively.)

step 4

Add the sliced patola (loofah) to the pot and stir-fry for a few minutes.

step 5

Pour in the chicken or vegetable broth and bring it to a simmer.

step 6

Add the miswa noodles to the pot and cook according to package instructions (usually just a few minutes until they are tender).

step 7

Season with fish sauce, salt, and pepper according to your taste.

step 8

Stir well and let it simmer for a few more minutes to allow the flavors to meld together.

step 9

Remove from heat and transfer to a serving bowl.

step 10

Garnish with chopped green onions, if desired.
