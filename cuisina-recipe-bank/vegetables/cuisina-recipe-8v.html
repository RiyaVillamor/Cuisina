Ginisang Munggo

Ginisang Munggo is a popular Filipino comfort food made with mung beans.

prep time

15 min.

cook time

35 min.

serving

1 serving

Tags : veggies

instructions

step 1

Rinse the mung beans (munggo) under running water. In a small pot, combine the mung beans and water. Bring to a boil and let it simmer for about 25-30 minutes or until the beans are tender. Set aside.

step 2

In a separate pan, heat the cooking oil over medium heat.

step 3

Saut√© the onion, garlic, and tomato until the onion is translucent and the tomatoes are softened.

step 4

Optional: If using pork belly or shrimp, add it to the pan and cook until browned or until the shrimp turns pink.

step 5

Add the cooked mung beans to the pan and mix well with the other ingredients.

step 6

Season with salt and pepper according to your taste.

step 7

Stir in the spinach or bitter melon leaves and cook until wilted.

step 8

Remove from heat and transfer to a serving dish and serve hot with steamed rice.
