Corned Beef Hash

Corned Beef Hash is a popular Filipino breakfast dish made from corned beef and diced potatoes. It is a hearty and flavorful meal that is quick and easy to prepare, making it a go-to option for busy mornings or anytime comfort food.

prep time

10 min.

cook time

15 min.

serving

1 serving

Tags : veggies

instructions

step 1

Heat the cooking oil in a pan over medium heat.

step 2

Saut√© the onion, bell pepper, and garlic until fragrant and slightly softened.

step 3

Add the corned beef to the pan and break it up using a spatula or fork.

step 4

Cook for a few minutes until the corned beef is heated through.

step 5

Add the diced potato to the pan and mix well with the corned beef mixture.

step 6

Season with salt and pepper according to your taste.

step 7

Cover the pan and cook for about 10-15 minutes, or until the potatoes are tender.

step 8

Stir occasionally to prevent sticking and ensure even cooking.

step 9

Once the potatoes are cooked, remove from heat and serve hot with a fried egg on top, if desired.
