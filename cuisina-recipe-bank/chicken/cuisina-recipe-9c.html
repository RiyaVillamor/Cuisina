Chicken Curry

Chicken Curry is a popular dish in the Philippines, reflecting the influence of Indian and Southeast Asian flavors.

prep time

10 min.

cook time

30 min.

serving

1 serving

Tags : chicken

instructions

step 1

Heat the cooking oil in a pan over medium heat. Saut√© the onion and garlic until fragrant.

step 2

Add the chicken pieces to the pan and cook until they are lightly browned on all sides.

step 3

Add the potatoes, and carrots to the pan. Stir and cook for a few minutes until the vegetables start to soften.

step 4

Sprinkle the curry powder over the chicken and vegetables. Stir well to coat.

step 5

Pour in the coconut milk and chicken broth. Bring it to a simmer and let it cook for about 15-20 minutes or until the chicken is cooked through and the vegetables are tender.

step 6

Season with salt and pepper according to your taste.

step 7

Remove from heat and let it cool slightly.

step 8

Serve the Chicken Curry hot with steamed rice.
