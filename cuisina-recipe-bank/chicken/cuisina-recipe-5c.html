Chicken Pastel

Chicken Pastel is a classic Filipino comfort food that showcases the fusion of Spanish and Filipino flavors. The dish features tender chicken and vegetables cooked in a creamy sauce, typically served inside a puff pastry shell.

prep time

10 min.

cook time

30 min.

serving

1 serving

Tags : chicken

instructions

step 1

Heat the cooking oil in a pan over medium heat. Saut√© the onion and garlic until fragrant.

step 2

Add the chicken pieces to the pan and cook until they are lightly browned on all sides.

step 3

Add the carrots, potatoes, green peas, and mushrooms to the pan. Stir and cook for a few minutes until the vegetables are slightly tender.

step 4

In a small bowl, mix the chicken broth, evaporated milk, and flour until well combined. Gradually pour this mixture into the pan, stirring constantly.

step 5

Bring the mixture to a simmer and cook for about 10-15 minutes, or until the sauce thickens and the chicken is cooked through. Stir occasionally.

step 6

Season with salt and pepper according to your taste.

step 7

Remove from heat and let it cool slightly.

step 8

Serve the Chicken Pastel hot, either by filling a puff pastry shell with the mixture or serving it over slices of bread.
