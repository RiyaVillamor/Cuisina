Adobong Manok

Adobong Manok is considered the national dish of the Philippines. It is a flavorful combination of chicken, soy sauce, and vinegar, simmered until tender.

prep time

30 min.

cook time

30 min.

serving

1 serving

Tags : chicken

instructions

step 1

In a bowl, marinate the chicken pieces with soy sauce and vinegar for at least 30 minutes.

step 2

Heat the cooking oil in a pan over medium heat.

step 3

Saut√© the minced garlic until fragrant and golden brown.

step 4

Add the marinated chicken pieces (reserve the marinade) to the pan and cook until lightly browned on all sides.

step 5

Pour in the reserved marinade, along with the bay leaf and water.

step 6

Bring the mixture to a simmer, then cover the pan and let it cook for about 30 minutes or until the chicken is tender and cooked through. Stir occasionally.

step 7

Season with salt and pepper according to your taste.

step 8

If desired, you can increase the heat to high and let the sauce reduce and thicken slightly.
