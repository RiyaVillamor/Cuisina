Sinampalukang Manok

Sinampalukang Manok is a traditional Filipino soup that derives its sour flavor from tamarind.

prep time

10 min.

cook time

40 min.

serving

1 serving

Tags : chicken

instructions

step 1

In a pot, bring water to a boil. Add the chicken pieces and cook until they are tender. Remove any scum that rises to the surface.

step 2

In a separate bowl, dissolve the tamarind pulp in 1/4 cup water. Strain the mixture to extract the tamarind juice.

step 3

Once the chicken is tender, add the tamarind juice, onion, garlic, and ginger to the pot. Simmer for 15-20 minutes to allow the flavors to meld.

step 4

In a small bowl, mix the heavy cream and flour until well combined. Gradually stir the cream mixture into the pan.

step 5

Add the kangkong leaves to the pot and cook for an additional 2-3 minutes until wilted.

step 6

Add the chopped cabbage to the pot and cook for an additional 5 minutes or until the cabbage is wilted and cooked through.

step 7

Season with salt and pepper according to your taste.

step 8

Serve hot and enjoy!
