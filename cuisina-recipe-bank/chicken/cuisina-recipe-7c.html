Buffalo Wings

Buffalo Wings, originally created in the United States, have gained popularity in the Philippines as a delicious and spicy appetizer.

prep time

10 min.

cook time

20 min.

serving

1 serving

Tags : chicken

instructions

step 1

In a bowl, mix together the flour, garlic powder, paprika, salt, and black pepper.

step 2

Coat the chicken wings in the flour mixture, shaking off any excess.

step 3

Heat cooking oil in a deep pan or fryer to about 350°F (175°C).

step 4

Fry the chicken wings in batches for 10-12 minutes or until they are golden brown and cooked through. Drain on a paper towel-lined plate.

step 5

In a separate bowl, combine the melted butter and hot sauce.

step 6

Toss the fried chicken wings in the sauce mixture until well coated.

step 7

Serve hot with ranch or blue cheese dressing and celery sticks on the side.
