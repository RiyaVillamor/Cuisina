Chicken Afritada

Chicken Afritada is a popular Filipino stew that showcases a blend of Spanish and Filipino flavors. The dish typically includes chicken, tomato sauce, and a variety of vegetables.

prep time

15 min.

cook time

45 min.

serving

1 serving

Tags : chicken

instructions

step 1

Heat the cooking oil in a pan over medium heat.

step 2

Saut√© the minced garlic and chopped onion until fragrant and translucent.

step 3

Add the chicken pieces and cook until lightly browned on all sides.

step 4

Pour in the tomato sauce and water. Stir well to coat the chicken.

step 5

Cover the pan and let the chicken simmer for about 20 minutes or until cooked through and tender.

step 6

Add the potato cubes, carrot slices, and green peas to the pan. Stir to combine.

step 7

Season with salt and pepper according to your taste.

step 8

Cover the pan again and let the vegetables cook for another 10-15 minutes or until they are tender.

step 9

Add the bell pepper strips and cook for an additional 2-3 minutes.

step 10

Remove from heat and transfer the Chicken Afritada to a serving plate.
