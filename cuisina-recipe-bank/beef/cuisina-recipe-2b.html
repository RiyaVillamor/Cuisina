Nilagang Baka

Nilagang Baka is a traditional Filipino dish that is often enjoyed during colder months. It is known for its simplicity, as it uses basic ingredients and relies on slow simmering to extract the flavors of the beef and vegetables.

prep time

10 min.

cook time

1 hr 30 min.

serving

1 serving

Tags : beef lunch

instructions

step 1

In a pot, add water and bring it to a boil.

step 2

Add the beef and onion to the pot. Simmer for about 1 hour or until the beef becomes tender.

step 3

Skim off any impurities that rise to the surface.

step 4

Add the potatoes, carrots, and cabbage to the pot. Continue to simmer for another 10-15 minutes or until the vegetables are cooked but still firm.

step 5

Season with salt and pepper to taste.

step 6

Remove from heat and serve hot.
