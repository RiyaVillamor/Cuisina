Balbacua

Balbacua is a hearty stew that originated from Cebu, Philippines. It is traditionally made with beef shank or oxtail, slowly cooked to tenderize the meat and enhance its rich flavors. The dish is known for its thick and savory broth, often enjoyed with rice or bread.

prep time

15 min.

cook time

2 hrs.

serving

1 serving

step 1

Stir in the soy sauce and oyster sauce, coating the meat evenly.

step 5

Pour in the water and bring it to a boil.

step 6

Reduce the heat to low, cover the pot, and let it simmer for about 2 hours or until the meat is tender.
