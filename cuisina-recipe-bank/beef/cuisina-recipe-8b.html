Beef with broccoli

Beef with Broccoli is a popular Chinese-inspired dish that has become a staple in Filipino cuisine. It showcases the harmonious combination of tender beef slices and vibrant broccoli florets in a savory sauce.

prep time

15 min.

cook time

15 min.

serving

1 serving

Tags : beef lunch

instructions

step 1

In a small bowl, combine the oyster sauce, soy sauce, and cornstarch (if using). Stir until well mixed. Set aside.

step 2

Heat the cooking oil in a pan or wok over medium heat.

step 3

Saut√© the onions and garlic until fragrant and translucent.

step 4

Add the beef slices to the pan and stir-fry until they are cooked to your desired level of doneness. Remove the beef from the pan and set aside.

step 5

In the same pan, add the broccoli florets and stir-fry for a few minutes until they turn bright green and are slightly tender.

step 6

Return the cooked beef to the pan and pour the sauce mixture over it. Stir-fry for a minute or until the sauce thickens.

step 7

Season with salt and pepper to taste.

step 8

Remove from heat and transfer to a serving plate.

step 9

Garnish with sesame seeds, if desired.
