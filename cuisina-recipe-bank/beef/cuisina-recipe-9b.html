Bistek Tagalog

Bistek Tagalog is a popular Filipino dish that combines the flavors of beef, soy sauce, and citrus juice. It is often referred to as the "Filipino version of beef steak." The dish is characterized by its savory and tangy taste.

prep time

10 min.

cook time

30 min.

serving

1 serving

Tags : beef lunch

instructions

step 1

In a bowl, combine the soy sauce and calamansi juice (or lemon juice). Mix well to create the marinade.

step 2

Add the thinly sliced beef to the marinade, ensuring that each piece is coated. Let it marinate for about 10 minutes.

step 3

Heat the cooking oil in a pan over medium heat.

step 4

Sauté the onions and garlic until they become translucent and fragrant.

step 5

Remove the beef from the marinade, reserving the marinade for later use.

step 6

Add the beef slices to the pan and cook until browned on both sides.

step 7

Pour the reserved marinade into the pan, and simmer for a few minutes to allow the flavors to meld together.

step 8

Season with salt and pepper to taste.

step 9

Remove from heat and transfer to a serving plate.

step 10

Garnish with some sautéed onions, if desired.
