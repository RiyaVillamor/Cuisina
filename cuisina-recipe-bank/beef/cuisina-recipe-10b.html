Bulalo

Bulalo is a popular Filipino soup dish that originated from the province of Batangas. It is known for its rich and savory broth made from beef marrow bones, simmered for hours to extract its flavors.

prep time

10 min.

cook time

1 hr 30 min.

serving

1 serving

Tags : beef lunch

instructions

step 1

In a pot, combine the beef marrow bone, water, chopped onion, and minced garlic.

step 2

Bring the mixture to a boil over high heat.

step 3

Reduce the heat to low, cover the pot, and simmer for about 1 hour or until the meat is tender and the flavors are infused into the broth

step 4

Skim off any impurities that rise to the surface of the broth.

step 5

Add the diced potato to the pot and continue simmering for about 15 minutes or until the potato is cooked and tender.

step 6

Add the chopped cabbage to the pot and cook for an additional 5 minutes or until the cabbage is wilted and cooked through.

step 7

Season with salt and pepper to taste. You can also add fish sauce for additional flavor, if desired.

step 8

Remove from heat and transfer to a serving bowl and serve hot and enjoy the flavorful Bulalo soup.
