Beef Kebab

Beef kebab, also known as "kebabs ng baka" in Filipino, is a popular street food in the Philippines. It is often enjoyed with a dipping sauce or served with rice and vegetables.

prep time

40 min.

cook time

8 min.

serving

1 serving

Tags : beef lunch dinner

Reminder

Note 1

If using bamboo skewers, soak them in water for 30 minutes to prevent burning.

instructions

step 1

In a bowl, combine the soy sauce, lemon juice, Worcestershire sauce, minced garlic, black pepper, paprika, salt, and sugar. Mix well to create the marinade.

step 2

Add the beef cubes to the marinade and toss to coat. Let the beef marinate for at least 30 minutes, or up to overnight in the refrigerator.

step 3

Preheat a grill or grill pan over medium-high heat.

step 4

Thread the marinated beef cubes onto the skewers, leaving a little space between each piece.

step 5

Brush the cooking oil onto the grill or grill pan to prevent sticking.

step 6

Place the skewers on the grill or grill pan and cook for about 3-4 minutes per side, or until the beef is cooked to your desired doneness.

step 7

Remove the beef kebabs from the grill and let them rest for a few minutes.
