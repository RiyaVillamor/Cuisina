Mechado

Mechado is a popular Filipino dish with Spanish influences. It is known for its tender beef chunks cooked in a flavorful tomato-based sauce.

prep time

15 min.

cook time

1 hr 30 min.

serving

1 serving

Tags : beef lunch

instructions

step 1

Heat the cooking oil in a pot over medium heat.

step 2

Saut√© the onions, garlic, and tomatoes until the onions become translucent.

step 3

Add the beef cubes to the pot and cook until browned on all sides.

step 4

Pour in the soy sauce and calamansi juice, and stir well to coat the meat.

step 5

Add the beef broth or water to the pot, and bring it to a boil.

step 6

Reduce the heat to low, cover the pot, and simmer for about 1 hour or until the meat is tender.

step 7

Add the potatoes, carrots, and red bell pepper to the pot. Continue simmering for another 15-20 minutes or until the vegetables are cooked but still firm.

step 8

Season with salt and pepper to taste.

step 5

Remove from heat and serve hot.
