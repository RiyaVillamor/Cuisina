Pork Adobo

Adobo is considered the national dish of the Philippines. It is a cooking technique where meat is marinated in a mixture of soy sauce and vinegar, then simmered until tender. Pork Adobo is a popular variation and is known for its rich, savory flavors.

prep time

10 min.

cook time

40 min.

serving

1 serving

Tags : pork

instructions

step 1

In a bowl, combine the pork cubes, minced garlic, soy sauce, vinegar, water, bay leaf, and black peppercorns. Marinate for at least 15 minutes.

step 2

Heat cooking oil in a pan over medium heat. Saut√© the marinated pork with the marinade until the meat is lightly browned.

step 3

Reduce the heat to low and cover the pan. Let it simmer for about 30 minutes or until the pork is tender and the sauce has thickened.

step 4

Stir occasionally to prevent sticking and ensure even cooking.

step 5

Adjust the seasoning with salt according to your preference.

step 6

Transfer the Pork Adobo to a serving plate.

step 7

Serve hot with steamed rice.
