Lechon Kawali

Lechon Kawali is a popular Filipino dish known for its crispy and flavorful pork belly. It is traditionally cooked by deep-frying the pork until it becomes golden and crispy on the outside while remaining tender on the inside. Lechon Kawali is often served during special occasions and gatherings, and it pairs well with a tangy vinegar dipping sauce.

prep time

10 min.

cook time

30 min.

serving

1 serving

Tags : pork

instructions

step 1

In a pot, combine the pork belly, water, salt, and black peppercorns. Bring to a boil and simmer for about 20 minutes or until the meat is tender.

step 2

Remove the pork belly from the pot and let it cool for a few minutes. Pat it dry with paper towels to remove excess moisture.

step 3

Heat cooking oil in a deep pan or pot over medium heat. Carefully add the pork pieces to the hot oil and fry until golden and crispy. Ensure the pork is fully submerged in the oil.

step 4

Remove the crispy pork from the oil and transfer it to a plate lined with paper towels to absorb excess oil. Let it cool slightly.

step 5

Season the Lechon Kawali with salt and pepper to taste.

step 6

Serve hot with steamed rice and vinegar dipping sauce.
