Tokwa't Baboy

Tokwat Baboy is a popular Filipino dish made with tofu (tokwa) and pork (baboy). The dish is known for its combination of crispy fried tofu and savory pork, seasoned with soy sauce and vinegar. It is a delicious and versatile dish that can be enjoyed as a main course or a side dish.

prep time

15 min.

cook time

15 min.

serving

1 serving

Tags : pork

instructions

step 1

In a pan, heat cooking oil over medium heat. Fry the tofu cubes until they turn golden brown. Remove and set aside.

step 2

In the same pan, saut√© minced garlic and chopped onion until fragrant and translucent.

step 3

Add the boiled pork belly cubes to the pan and stir-fry for a few minutes.

step 4

Pour in soy sauce, vinegar, sugar, and ground black pepper. Mix well and simmer for 2-3 minutes.

step 5

Add the fried tofu cubes and toss gently to coat with the sauce. Cook for an additional 2 minutes.

step 6

Remove from heat and transfer the Tokwat Baboy to a serving plate.

step 7

Garnish with chopped spring onions.

step 8

Serve hot as an appetizer or with steamed rice.
