Bicol Express

Bicol Express is a popular Filipino dish hailing from the Bicol region, known for its spicy and flavorful cuisine. The dish features pork cooked in coconut milk, flavored with shrimp paste and spiced with chili peppers. It is a must-try for those who enjoy bold and fiery flavors.

prep time

10 min.

cook time

25 min.

serving

1 serving

Tags : beef seafood veggies pork

instructions

step 1

Heat cooking oil in a pan over medium heat. Saut√© minced garlic and chopped onion until fragrant and translucent.

step 2

Add the pork strips to the pan and cook until lightly browned.

step 3

Add the chopped red chili pepper and stir-fry for a minute.

step 4

Stir in the shrimp paste and cook for another minute to release its flavors.

step 5

Pour in the coconut milk and bring to a simmer. Let it cook for about 15 minutes or until the sauce thickens.

step 6

Season with salt to taste. Adjust the seasoning according to your preference

step 7

Remove from heat and transfer the Bicol Express to a serving plate.

step 8

Serve hot with steamed rice.
